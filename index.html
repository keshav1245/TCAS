About

Permanence of the stars hundreds of thousands

When it comes to your dreams, every risk is worth it


With the above thought in mind 6 people from varied fields got together and after several meetings finally in the year 2004 formed a club called “Team Chandigarh Adventure Sports”- TCAS. The team was just perfect as each of them was a specialized professional of his field. The club was prepared to host any kind of Motorsport Event. Over this long period, the club has made its mark in almost all fields like

Autocross & Motocross: - "Never become a copy of someone else, ALWAYS REMAIN ORIGINAL" with this moto we created the first ever dirt twin flat track with a bridge cross, founded the "The TCAS OYA AutoX, MotoX cum Off-road Challenge" in 2007 and are running it as an annual event under various banners. TCAS has further graduated to be the organizer of the Chandigarh round of the National Autocross in 2016 and 2017.

Single Stage Rallies: - In the year 2012 TCAS along with another club created an event called ‘Speed Sprint’. This event had all the 3 elements of a rally that is Tarmac, Dirt & Gravel. This event is running successfully till now.

Promotional events: - TCAS has made a niche in Automobile promotional events with many reputed brands like Mahindra, Chevrolet, Renault, Isuzu etc.

TSD Rallies & Vintage car rallies: - TCAS has organized several TSD rallies - General, Theme Specific and for various Social Causes. TCAS organizes a women's car rally every year on the occassion of International Women's Day. This rally is the biggest women's car rally of Chandigarh. The event was later clubbed with a Vintage and a Classic car rally which has become a lifestyle event since the year 2013. In the year 2012 TCAS partnered with Chandigarh Police to organize an event called ‘The Rally Of Chandigarh’ for a cause to obey traffic rules and safe driving. This particular event had more than 700 enthusiasts participating on their rally cars, rally bikes, super cars, super bikes, vintage and classic cars etc.

Off-roading: - We at TCAS will always be proud to have created the first competitive off-roading event of North India called ‘The UNCAGED’. This event is conducted on a specially created track which consists of obstacles of International standards. Other than this we have organized Mahindra Great Escape several times.

Several other tailor-made corporate events are part of our calendar every year like T.I.E. Chandigarh Chapter off-road trail drives, DLF women on wheels etc.

To sum it up we believe that:-
“Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts”
With a commitment to make history we go on……….
